MEET THE ARTIST
Mustache Boii

Mustache Boii was created a few years back. It was a sketch given by someone special and was later developed by the artist as his own character.

The character Mustache Boii is the artist himself, a long-haired guy with a nevus in his lower left eye and of course, the legendary mustache.

With the idea of becoming whoever we want to be, he started creating different mustaches unique to each other, from colors to mood to concept.

It's a never-ending creative collaboration between the artist, what he sees, what he thinks, and what he feels.
